Double Care Adult Day Center

Double Care Adult Day Services (DCADS) located near the Big World Supermarket at 2900 Cottman Street, Northeastern Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, is the first large-scale elderly day care center founded and managed by Chinese in Northeastern Philadelphia. It is a new facility with spacious, bright and comfortable venues and convenient transportation. There are dining areas, activity areas, bathing rooms, nurses' diagnosis rooms, social service rooms, etc., and arrange rich and colorful Chinese entertainment activities for the elderly. Every day, there will be a special car to pick up and drop off the elderly and provide rich Chinese-style dishes.


The center serves Chinese-aged elders with a Chinese identity; there are registered nurses and carers who care for the health of the elderly. The service staff are fluent in Mandarin, Cantonese, Fuzhounese, and English, and can help the elderly in the center to communicate externally. Providing a home for the elderly and giving them more sense of belonging, Double Care Adult Day Services (DCADS) will bring the gospel to the elderly in the community. If you want to be safe and socialize with other Chinese seniors, come to Double Care Adult Day Services (DCADS). We will have enthusiastic staff to help. Fill in the application form; the center will provide free transportation and financial assistance.
